Solution Analyst
Job Description

Job Title : Solution Analyst


Job Responsibilities :

  • Develop high performance mobile and web applications in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Android, iOS and various frontend and backend technologies.
  • Create requirements, design documentation and test plans for above, adhering to standard templates provided by the organization.
  • Write clean, maintainable, efficient code that confirms to software development standard.
  • Well versed with multiple front end and backend technologies such as Angular, BootStrap, Node, PHP, Spring, ReactJS, etc.
  • Understand full stack development process and is familiar with REST.
  • Can interact with client directly on requirement gathering, delivery planning and implementation.

Desired Skills :

  • Ability to learn and implement multiple technologies required for Software development fast.

Qualification :

M.Tech, B.Tech, M.Sc, B.Sc, BCA, BE, ME (COMP and IT students only) and MCA.

Job Description

Title : UI/UX Designer


Job Responsibilities :

  • Are looking for designers having good creativity and communication, who are passionate about delivering a well-designed, scalable, intuitive user interface.
  • Motivated to learn things through sharing and experimentation that will help them and company become better at creating solutions.
  • Promotes a humble behaviour and maintains curiosity in learning new things to take on new challenges.
  • Developing user experience for web apps/mobile apps (iOS/Android).

  • Screen design of new websites or mobile applications using design tools like Photoshop, illustrator, XD etc.
  • Wireframe and UI prototyping.
  • Capability to communicate with clients, provide insight into UX decisions and collaborate with the project team.
  • Coordinate with engineering teams to develop great user experiences and ensure designs are optimized and implemented effectively.
  • Please share your portfolio for the same.

Desired Skills :

  • Having good team spirit and strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders.
  • Creative, resourceful, detail-oriented, highly organized, hands-on.
  • Bring original ideas and fresh approaches to each project.

Qualification :

Bachelor of Design, Master of Design, Bachelor of Fine Arts in Applied Arts, Valid Certification Course in UI/UX Designing.

Human Resource
Job Description

Title : Human Resource


Job Responsibilities :

  • Establishes recruiting requirements by studying organization plans and objectives; meeting with managers to discuss needs.
  • Builds applicant sources by researching and contacting community services, colleges, employment agencies, recruiters, media, and internet sites; providing organization information, opportunities, and benefits; making presentations; taking induction.
  • Customer centricity is a core value that drives us to be more responsive in communication, be proactive in taking accountability of our work, be very organized in planning, and be agile in responding to constant changes in the project.
  • Attracts applicants by placing job advertisements; contacting recruiters, using job sites.
  • Determines applicant qualifications by interviewing applicants; analysing responses; verifying references; comparing qualifications to job requirements.
  • Arranges management interviews by coordinating schedules.
  • Evaluates applicants by discussing job requirements and applicant qualifications with managers; interviewing applicants on consistent set of qualifications.
  • Improves organization attractiveness by recommending new policies and practices; monitoring job offers and compensation practices; emphasizing benefits and perks.
  • Avoids legal challenges by understanding current legislation; enforcing regulations with managers; recommending new procedures; conducting training.
  • Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.
  • Contributing to the development of HR department goals, objectives, and systems Conducting new employee orientations and employee relations counselling.
  • Completing exit formalities of employee.
  • Maintaining department records and reports.

Desired Skills :

  • Ability to learn and implement multiple technologies required for Software development fast.
  • Proficiency in MS-Office-word, excel & PowerPoint.
  • Positive attitude having a good leading skill.

Qualification :

M.Tech, B.Tech, M.Sc, B.Sc, BCA, BE, ME (COMP and IT students only) and MCA
